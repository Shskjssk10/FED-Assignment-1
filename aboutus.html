ABOUT US

OUR AIM

Provide quality with a variety

At Saizeriya, we know that variety is a key part of a restaurant's appeal. We aim for excellence by providing a wide variety of choices to our valued diners. For all occasions, whether busy or as an occasional luxury, Saizeriya is dedicated to providing top-quality meals, taking into consideration the diversity of lifestyles and preferences. We at Saizeriya position ourselves as a "Casual Italian Restaurant" that welcomes repeated visits with family members, friends, couples, and more, throughout their daily lives. We will continue to bring you the Italian style food culture, and to delight you on all occasions.

CONCEPT

Making Saizeriya Your Everyday Diner

We want to make Saizeriya peoples everyday go-to diner, your go-to diner. We are actively developing our restaurant chain. We have about 30 stores in Singapore, more than 1000 in our home country of Japan, and about 400 locations in China, Hong Kong and Taiwan. Our mission is to open up more stores within easy reach for more diners like you to visit in Singapore.

OUR HISTORY

The Very Beginning...

Started as a man's dream in 1967 in a small space above a grocery store at a shop house in Japan, Saizeriya has now expanded to over 1,000 restaurants in Japan, and has established a strong presence in many cities in Asia such as Beijing Guangzhou, Shanghai, Hong Kong, Taiwan, and of course, Singapore.
